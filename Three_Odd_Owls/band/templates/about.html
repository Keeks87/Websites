About the Band

Three Odd Owls is a rock trance band formed in 2017. The band consists of three members, Alex Johnson (guitar), Mia Rodriguez (bass), and Max Patel (drums). Their music is characterized by a unique blend of rock and trance, which creates an immersive and mesmerizing sound. They have released several singles and one EP, and have gained a following for their high-energy live performances. Three Odd Owls is known for their intricate instrumental arrangements, catchy hooks, and thought-provoking lyrics. Overall, they are a band that pushes the boundaries of traditional rock music and creates an unforgettable experience for their listeners.

Alex Johnson (guitar)

Alex Johnson is a classically trained guitarist with a passion for rock music. He began playing guitar at the age of 8 and has since honed his skills in a variety of styles, from blues to metal. Alex's unique approach to guitar playing combines technical proficiency with a keen sense of melody, resulting in the intricate and memorable riffs that are a hallmark of Three Odd Owls' sound.

Mia Rodriguez (bass)

Mia Rodriguez is a self-taught bassist who discovered her love for music at a young age. She grew up listening to a wide range of genres, from jazz to hip hop, and eventually found her niche in the world of rock. Mia's bass lines provide the foundation for Three Odd Owls' trance-inspired sound, and her energetic stage presence keeps audiences on their feet.

Max Patel (drums)

Max Patel is a multi-instrumentalist who brings a diverse musical background to Three Odd Owls. He began playing drums at the age of 12 and has since expanded his skills to include guitar, piano, and even the tabla. Max's dynamic drumming style adds an extra layer of intensity to the band's live shows, and his ability to seamlessly integrate elements of world music into their sound is a testament to his versatility as a musician.
